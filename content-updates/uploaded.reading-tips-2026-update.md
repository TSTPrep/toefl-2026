## TIP #2: FIND YOUR KEYWORDS AND FOCUS ON THEM

Look for the main idea of the paragraph. It's usually in the first or last sentence. Circle it. This saves you TONS of time later because:
- You know what the paragraph is about before reading every word
- When answering questions, you already have context
- You can quickly eliminate wrong answers

**2026 Application**: This keyword strategy works especially well for **Task 2: Read in Daily Life** where you need to scan emails, ads, or forms quickly for specific information. Don't read every word - let the keywords guide you to the answer.

You will be given approximately **27 minutes** to complete the TOEFL Reading section in the 2026 format, and that's a significantly faster pace than the old test.

- **Module 1**: ~11-12 minutes (establishes your level)

**Critical 2026 Note**: The adaptive system means you can't afford to skip questions or random guess. Each answer in Module 1 affects your Module 2 difficulty, so use educated guesses and elimination strategies rather than random selection.
